How to use the checkboxes: Check a meal to exclude it from your reimbursement — only mark meals that were provided to you during your actual travel window (after your departure time on the first day, before your return time on the last day). Examples: a conference lunch, a hosted dinner, or a meal included in a registration fee. The tool automatically calculates the deduction using GSA meal values. The first and last days of travel are reimbursed at 75% of the daily rate. Meals shown in gray are outside your travel window and cannot be claimed.
